Chime In!

Introduction

Skip straight to the start screen

Set Up

  1. Add Chime In to Teams. (You can sideload this Manifest file)
  2. Add "Incoming Webhook" to the channel you want to track.
    (Channel -> More Options -> Connectors -> Incoming webhook)
  3. To send a message to a rider, just mention @Chime In

Using Chime In While you ride

  1. Enter your name and webhook endpoint in the Chime In start screen
  2. Click Start
  3. Let your channel know that you're going for a ride
  4. Keep Chime In messages screen open on your phone
  5. You'll hear the messages read out
  6. Reply by ringing your bike bell (clapping works too)
    One ring sends a 'like'. See what more rings gives you.
